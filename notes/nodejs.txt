To Update to the Newest Versions of the Packages But Under the Constriant of package.json
----------------------
$ npm update

To Update to the Newest Versions of the Packages But Without the Constriant of package.json
----------------------
$ npm i -g npm-check-updates

Run npm-check-updates to update all version numbers in your 
package.json to the latest versions:

$ ncu -u

$ npm i
